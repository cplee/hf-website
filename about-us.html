Meet the Partners.

Estee Horn
Partner & CPA
Estée Horn was born and raised in San Jose, CA. Estée began her career in public accounting in 1995 at a large San Jose firm while attending college at San Jose State. Upon graduating from SJSU in 1998 (B.S. in Business Administration with a concentration in Accounting), Estée accepted a position with Ernst & Young, where she completed her audit hours working on engagements for large publicly traded companies. Estée became a licensed CPA in 2001. After Ernst & Young, Estée obtained a large breadth of experience in both public accounting and private industry, including going through an IPO at a high tech start-up and running the accounting and finance department for a commercial general contracting business.  Estée moved to El Dorado Hills in 2003 and began working in the Folsom area. Estée has focused on taxation and small business accounting since 2005.    
Erin Fulton
Partner & CPA
Erin Fulton grew up in Springfield, Missouri. In 2000, she graduated with a Masters of Accounting from the University of Kansas and moved to Folsom, California. She proceeded to work at a large public accounting firm in Sacramento while earning her CPA and studying taxation at Golden Gate University. She met Estee in 2013 and has been working with her ever since. She loves spending time with her husband and two children and enjoys opportunities to volunteer in the classroom and in other activities. Travelling is her passion and she takes every opportunity to explore new destinations.
